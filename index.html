Where does the old way actually lose time?
⏳
On hold & waiting
Every supplier call involves hold time — often 3–5 minutes before anyone picks up. With 4 suppliers per quote, that's up to 20 minutes of dead time before a single price is on paper.
📝
Manual comparison
Comparing prices across 4 suppliers means reading 4 different notepads part by part. Errors creep in. The fastest price doesn't always win — the most legible scribble does.
🔁
Double-handling every order
After all the quoting, you still have to call the winning suppliers back to actually place the order — and manually create a PO. Every job touches the phone at least twice per supplier.
What PartsCheck removes from the process entirely
✓
Hold music & waiting
Suppliers receive the quote digitally and respond in their own time. Your staff do other work while prices come in.
✓
Reading parts aloud
Parts are entered once, digitally. No mis-heard part numbers, no having to repeat yourself three times.
✓
Manual price comparison
All supplier responses are ranked side-by-side automatically. Best price is always visible at a glance.
✓
Callback to place orders
One-click ordering from within the platform. PO is auto-generated and sent — no second round of calls.
✓
Lost paper trails
Every quote, every supplier response, every PO is captured digitally. No lost notepads, no disputes.
✓
Information asymmetry
Every supplier sees the same request. You see all responses together. Pricing becomes transparent and competitive.
